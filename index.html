For the for swirl, melt butter iver meduim heat in a sauce pan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss into a saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over meduim heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chuncky -jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside, and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over meduim heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until the sugar is completely dissovled and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks.Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is completly cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and proccess according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to prpcess for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
